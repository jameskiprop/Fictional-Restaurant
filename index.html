Beverages

Our beverages (Traditional, Fruit and Alcoholic)

Traditional beverages
  • Chai (Kenyan Tea): A blend of black tea, milk, sugar, and spices like ginger and cardamom.

  • Dawa: made with honey, lime, sugar, and vodka, served over ice.

  • Uji (Porridge): A fermented cereal porridge made from millet, sorghum, or maize, often sweetened and served warm.

Main dishes

Our main course meals

  • Nyama Choma: Grilled meat, often goat or beef, served with kachumbari

  • Pilau: cooked with meat (chicken, beef, or goat)

  • Samaki wa Kupaka: Fish cooked in a rich coconut sauce, typically served with rice or ugali.
